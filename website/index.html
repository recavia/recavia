05 — LOCAL BY DESIGN

Your meeting memory
stays yours.

Dahlia stores meetings in a local SQLite database and processes speech on your Mac. AI receives context only when you ask a question or generate a summary. External clients get read-only access to the Vault you explicitly select.

◉

Local meeting database

Transcripts, summaries, screenshots, and notes are organized in Dahlia on your Mac.

⌁

On-device transcription

Apple Speech turns conversation into text locally while you record.

⌂

Scoped, read-only access

MCP exposes only meeting search, stored summaries, and confirmed original transcripts in the selected Vault.
